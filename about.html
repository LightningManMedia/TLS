Frequently Asked Questions

What is TSL?

TSL is a live and on-demand streaming service that give sports fans around the world affordable access to sports anytime, anywhere. Sign up today to stream your favorite events live and on demand on all your devices, only with the TSL app. You get exclusive MMA matchups from Domination hand-picked by reigning UFC Lightweight Champion, Khabib Nurmagomedov.

How can I watch?

  • TSL works on most smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, computers and gaming consoles
  • Everything streams at the best quality your device can handle (up to full HD 1080p)
  • Set reminders, pause and rewind live events
  • Watch on up to two devices at the same time
